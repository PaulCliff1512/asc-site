Servicing FAQs

How often should I service my car?

Most vehicles need a full service every 12 months or 12,000 miles, and an interim service every 6 months.

Will servicing my car maintain my warranty?

Yes — we use manufacturer‑approved parts and follow official service schedules.

What’s the difference between a full and major service?

A major service includes everything in a full service plus spark plugs, brake fluid and additional component checks.
